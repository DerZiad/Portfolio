Intern and working student for my practical project, in the project PV-eCarPort

The PV-eCarPort project signifies a groundbreaking initiative aimed at seamlessly integrating and managing the charging infrastructure for electric vehicles (eCars) and electric bikes (eBikes) at the SIJ company. This ambitious project introduces a sophisticated system that facilitates the manipulation of four eCars and eBikes charging stations, ensuring the seamless monitoring and control of data related to this project.

The core objective of this project is to establish an advanced charging ecosystem that maximizes the utilization of renewable energy sources, with a particular emphasis on photovoltaic (PV) power generated by solar panels. At the heart of this project lies a state-of-the-art system that leverages cutting-edge technology to enable real-time monitoring of critical charging parameters, including charging status, energy consumption, and battery health.

Through comprehensive data visualization, the SIJ company gains invaluable insights to inform decision-making regarding energy management, charging schedules, and overall operational efficiency. Additionally, the PV-eCarPort project incorporates predictions for the power produced by the PV system using Dymola, further enhancing the accuracy and reliability of the charging process. The integration of the PV eCarPort Diagram Observations system allows the visualization of the actual state of power charging, ensuring optimal utilization of available resources.

To enhance user experience and convenience, the PV-eCarPort project offers an API that integrates with an iOS application designed for customer use. This application enables users to register, sign in, and seamlessly access the charging stations. Moreover, it facilitates transactions, promoting a streamlined and user-friendly experience.

The PV-eCarPort project takes pride in its commitment to providing a user-friendly interface, ensuring that users can effortlessly navigate and interact with the system. This emphasis on user-centric design promotes accessibility and fosters a positive user experience.

In summary, the PV-eCarPort project represents an innovative initiative that integrates and manages the charging infrastructure for eCars and eBikes at the SIJ company. With its focus on renewable energy utilization, real-time monitoring, and user-friendly interfaces, the project is poised to revolutionize the way electric vehicles are charged, promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility.
