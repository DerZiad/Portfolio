mgm technology partners - Germany

Oct, 2023 - Feb, 2024

Intern and working student for Bachelor's degree thesis

Beginning my role as a working student at MGM in October, I committed myself to grasping the intricacies of MGM's tools, technologies, and frameworks. Initially, I immersed myself in learning Kotlin, the primary language employed by the workflow team. This foundational knowledge paved the way for me to utilize Spring Boot with Kotlin seamlessly.

As my journey unfolded, I ventured into A12 Modelisation, delving into the nuances of model construction. This phase of my learning journey expanded my proficiency, allowing me to effectively grasp the orchestration of processes using Camunda. Bringing it all together, I culminated my experiences by undertaking a comprehensive project. This endeavor involved integrating all the acquired knowledge into creating a full-stack demo project. Through this hands-on application, I showcased my ability in modelization, process orchestration, and utilizing various technologies gleaned from my time at MGM.

In December, I transitioned into a pivotal phase of my academic journey by initiating my bachelor's thesis in collaboration with MGM. The focus of my thesis was the integration of a payment system into a model-based low-code platform. This project aimed to streamline and enhance the platform's functionalities. In other words, every A12 modeler can integrate a payment checkout form using only the built payment sub-process. I strategically employed PayPal and Stripe as the designated payment providers to achieve this objective. These industry-leading platforms were chosen for their reliability and efficiency in handling online transactions.

Throughout the process, I've got help from mgm team to navigate the intricacies of the low-code platform and ensure seamless integration. The project allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge gained during my academic journey and provided an opportunity to contribute practically to a real-world, industry-specific challenge.

In conclusion, my journey at MGM as a working student has been marked by continuous learning and practical application, culminating in a significant contribution to my bachelor's thesis. Integrating a payment system into a model-based low-code platform showcases my technical skills and reflects my commitment to delivering impactful solutions in a professional field.
